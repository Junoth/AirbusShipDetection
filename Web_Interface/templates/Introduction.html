Data Process

Our model is based on a dataset with more than 60000 images.There are total more than 230000 ships on these images.The dataset is offered by kaggle.We use pandas to get all masks and use sklearn to split train set and validation set.In order to increase the space diversity,we use the image processing model in keras to pre-process all images like rotating,fliping,etc.

Transfer Learning

We use transfer learning to determine the probability of ships.Based on keras,two different models-VGG16 and VGG19 are used as pre-trained model.The prediction layer of these models are deleted and additional layers are added like FC layer and Drop-out layer to develop the performance.

Position detection

To get the location of ships in the image,we choose two models:Unet and Faster-RCNN.Images generated from these two models will be included in the final output.
